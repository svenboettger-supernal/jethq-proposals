Workspace · 08

Calendars

The primary company calendar. It replaces the Microsoft calendar no one uses and the priorities the team trades back and forth over WhatsApp.

01 / Overview

The brief

The team does not use the Microsoft calendar. They share priorities over a WhatsApp channel, which is hard to track and easy to lose. Calendars gives the company one shared calendar inside Flight Deck, so schedules and priorities live in one place.

It stays simple and fast, because the reason the current calendar goes unused is friction.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • One shared company calendar that the whole team actually uses.
  • Who is in and out is visible at a glance.
  • Priorities live in one place, not scattered across chat.
  • Sensible integration with the calendars the team already keeps.
  • Simple and fast to use, so friction never sends the team back to WhatsApp.

03 / Scope

Scope of work
