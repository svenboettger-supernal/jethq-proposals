Company Timeline

A live feed of what is happening across the company. New listings, closed deals, and other milestones, so the team shares a sense of momentum.

01 / Overview

The brief

Company Timeline is a live activity feed across Flight Deck. As listings go live, deals close, and milestones happen, they appear here, giving the whole team a shared, current picture without anyone assembling it.

It draws from the surfaces already in Flight Deck, mainly the Hub and Sales Ops, so it stays accurate with no extra work.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • A live activity feed runs across the company, current at a glance.
  • New listings and closed deals surface automatically as they happen.
  • Milestones across the business are visible in one place.
  • The team shares a sense of momentum from what the feed shows.
  • Low effort and high visibility, with nothing to maintain by hand.

03 / Scope

Scope of work

Stage 1 · Phase 5

The live feed with core events

Stand up the feed and get the core events flowing, new listings and closed deals.

  • Build the live activity feed surface inside Flight Deck.
  • Surface new listings automatically as they go live.
  • Surface closed deals automatically as they close.
  • Draw from the Hub and Sales Ops so the feed stays accurate.

Stage 2 · Phase 5

More event types and filtering

Broaden what the feed shows and let the team focus it.

  • Add more event types beyond listings and closed deals.
  • Surface additional milestones across the company.
  • Add simple filtering so each person can narrow the feed.
