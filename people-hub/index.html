Workspace · 04

People Hub

A living org chart and team directory. Who is on which team, what their role is, and how long they have been with the company. Modeled on a directory Supernal has built before.

01 / Overview

The brief

The team wants a simple people hub, a pseudo org chart plus general information such as team, role, and tenure. It helps everyone know who to go to and is a natural companion to onboarding.

It stays light on purpose. Sensitive HR and compensation data is not here, that stays on OneDrive with leadership.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • A team directory with role and tenure, so everyone can find who they need.
  • A pseudo org chart that shows how teams and reporting lines fit together.
  • See who is on which team at a glance, without asking around.
  • A simple profile per person that carries the essentials and nothing more.
  • Kept current without a spreadsheet, so the directory stays accurate on its own.

03 / Scope

Scope of work

Stage 1 · Phase 2

Directory and profiles

Stand up the directory with team, role, and tenure, and a profile per person.

  • The directory with team, role, and tenure for everyone.
  • A simple profile per person that carries the essentials.
  • Search and filter by team and role.
  • Web and mobile, so the directory travels with the team.

Stage 2 · Phase 2

Org chart and maintenance

Add the org-chart view and make the directory easy to keep current.

  • The org-chart view built from teams and reporting lines.
  • Simple maintenance so a named operator can keep it current.
  • New people appear as they join, without a spreadsheet.
