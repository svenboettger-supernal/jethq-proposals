Package 04

Ask Jetty

Jetty is JetHQ's own AI copilot, a friendly captain in a pilot's cap who lives inside Flight Deck. It is the everyday assistant and the company's second brain in one. General help, drafting, grammar, and translation happen here, so the team stops switching to ChatGPT or Claude, and because Jetty also knows JetHQ's own data, plain-language answers come grounded and sourced from across the platform: listings, SOPs, people, deals, and documents. The live company activity feed sits right alongside, so new listings and closed deals show up as they happen.

01 / Overview

The brief

Jetty is the Intelligence layer, given a face and a name. It sits on top of the whole Workspace and answers questions across it, a hive-mind or second brain for the company, presented as JetHQ's own copilot rather than a blank chat box. Ask in plain language and get an answer sourced from listings, SOPs, people, deals, and documents. The pilot persona is deliberate: it makes the assistant feel like part of the crew, tasteful and unmistakably JetHQ's own.

Jetty is also the general assistant the team reaches for all day: everyday help, drafting, grammar, and translation happen right here, so no one needs to switch to ChatGPT or Claude for routine work. What sets it apart is that it also knows JetHQ's own data, so answers are grounded in the company's listings, SOPs, people, deals, and documents rather than generic. A private-model path keeps sensitive work local. Alongside the assistant sits the live company activity feed, so the team sees new listings, closed deals, and milestones across the company as they happen.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • Plain-language questions are answered from across Flight Deck.
  • Answers are sourced so the team can trust them.
  • Coverage spans listings, SOPs, people, deals, and documents.
  • Jetty is JetHQ's own copilot, a named persona the team recognizes.
  • General help, translation, and drafting happen inside the platform, not in ChatGPT or Claude.
  • A private-model path handles sensitive work.
  • A live company activity feed surfaces new listings, closed deals, and milestones as they happen.
