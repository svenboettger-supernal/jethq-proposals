Package 06

Ask JetHQ & Activity

The company second brain, plus the live activity feed. Ask a question in plain language and get a sourced answer from across Flight Deck: listings, SOPs, people, deals, and documents, and see new listings and closed deals as they happen. This is where Marcus lives.

01 / Overview

The brief

Ask JetHQ is the Intelligence layer. It sits on top of the whole Workspace and answers questions across it, a hive-mind or second brain for the company. Ask in plain language and get an answer sourced from listings, SOPs, people, deals, and documents. This is the home for Marcus, the AI Employee.

It also covers the everyday language work the team does in a separate tab today, translation, grammar, and drafting, so that work stays inside the platform. Be honest about the value: this will not beat the public models at open-ended tasks, its value is that it knows JetHQ's own data and keeps it in one place. A private-model path keeps sensitive work local. Alongside the chatbot sits the live company activity feed, so the team sees new listings, closed deals, and milestones across the company as they happen.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • Plain-language questions are answered from across Flight Deck.
  • Answers are sourced so the team can trust them.
  • Coverage spans listings, SOPs, people, deals, and documents.
  • Ask JetHQ is the home for Marcus, the AI Employee.
  • Everyday translation and drafting happen inside the platform.
  • A private-model path handles sensitive work.
  • A live company activity feed surfaces new listings, closed deals, and milestones as they happen.
