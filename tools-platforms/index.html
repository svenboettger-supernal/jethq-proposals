Workspace · 07

Tools & Platforms

A vendor and license portal. Every tool and platform the company pays for, with its renewal date, a link to the contract, and a link to the platform itself. For the solutions engineers and internal IT.

01 / Overview

The brief

License and vendor spend is hard to track today. This module is a vendor management portal: each tool with a link to the platform, a link to the contract, and a renewal date, so nothing renews by surprise.

It gives the solutions engineers and internal IT one shared view of what the company pays for and when each contract comes up.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • One list of every vendor and license the company pays for.
  • Renewal dates visible for every tool, so nothing lapses without notice.
  • A link to the contract and a link to the platform on every tool.
  • A reminder before each renewal comes due.
  • An owner named on every tool.
