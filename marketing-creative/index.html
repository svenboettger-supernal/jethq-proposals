Workspace · 06

Marketing & Creative

All things marketing and creative in one place. The brand book, the templates, and the creative assets, unified from Microsoft 365 and Canva so the team works from a single library.

01 / Overview

The brief

Marketing assets are split between Microsoft 365 and Canva today. Supernal already has access to the brand book. The goal is one searchable library: brand identity, templates, and assets, with links to Canva where the source lives there.

This is a priority for the marketing lead, and it is a clean early-to-mid win because much of the material already exists and just needs unifying.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • One searchable marketing and brand library that holds everything in a single place.
  • The brand book front and center, so the identity is the first thing the team reaches.
  • Templates unified from Microsoft 365 and Canva into one set.
  • Canva links where the source is in Canva, so the team lands on the live file.
  • Find the right asset fast, without hunting across two platforms.
  • A simple flow to add and update assets so the library stays current.

03 / Scope

Scope of work

Stage 1 · Phase 3

Structure and the brand identity kit

Set up the library structure and bring the brand book in.

  • Stand up the library structure for brand identity, templates, and assets.
  • Bring the brand book in and put it front and center.
  • Establish the brand identity kit as the anchor of the surface.

Stage 2 · Phase 3

Templates and assets unified

Unify templates and assets from Microsoft 365 and Canva, with search.

  • Unify templates from Microsoft 365 and Canva into one set.
  • Bring creative assets together in the same library.
  • Add Canva links where the source lives in Canva.
  • Search across the full library so the right asset is fast to find.

Stage 3 · Phase 3

Add, update, and keep current

Give the team a simple flow to add and update assets and keep Canva links current.

  • A simple flow to add new assets to the library.
  • A simple flow to update assets already in the library.
  • Keep Canva links current as the source files change.
