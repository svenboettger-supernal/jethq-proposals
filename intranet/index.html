Package 03

Intranet, Knowledge & Marketing

The company intranet, rebuilt and modernized inside Flight Deck. SOP library, links and tools, the knowledge base, people, onboarding, the marketing and brand library, the vendor and license portal, and the company calendar. One place, so the team stops toggling between SharePoint, Microsoft 365, Monday, Canva, and more.

01 / Overview

The brief

Today the company's internal knowledge and operations are spread across a SharePoint intranet, Microsoft 365, Monday, Canva, and more. The current SharePoint intranet has grown hard to navigate, with content duplicated and dated. This package rebuilds it modern inside Flight Deck so the team never has to leave the platform to find what it needs.

The SOP library is one part of a bigger whole. The intranet also carries the links and tools the team uses every day (JetHQ links, industry tools, and listing platforms), the crew with an org chart, roles, and tenure, the onboarding and general biz-ops flows run in Monday today, the marketing and brand asset library unified from Microsoft 365 and Canva, a vendor and license portal for the solutions engineers and internal IT, and a company calendar for time off and holidays. Sensitive HR and compensation data stays on OneDrive with leadership.

Two points are worth setting out. First, Flight Deck replaces Monday with simple task and ticket management built in: create a task or ticket, assign an owner, set a due date, and move it through clear stages, to do, in progress, and done, on a board or list, with comments and attachments. The current tickets and flows come across in a one-time migration, with no ongoing sync back to Monday. Second, a company calendar brings time off and holidays into one view, with a simple vacation intake form so the team requests time off inside Flight Deck and everyone can see who is out.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • The SharePoint intranet rebuilt and modernized inside Flight Deck, one place the team never leaves.
  • Links and tools in one navigable place: JetHQ links, industry tools, and listing platforms.
  • A searchable knowledge and SOP library migrated from Microsoft 365.
  • The crew: an org chart, roles, and tenure.
  • Simple task and ticket management built into Flight Deck, replacing Monday: tasks and tickets with an owner, a due date, and stages from to do to done, plus comments and attachments. Onboarding and biz-ops flows move over in a one-time migration with no ongoing sync.
  • The marketing and brand asset library unified from Microsoft 365 and Canva, brand book front and center.
  • A vendor and license portal with renewal dates, contract links, and platform links, for the solutions engineers and IT.
  • A company calendar for time off and holidays, with a simple vacation intake form so the team requests time off and everyone can see who is out.
