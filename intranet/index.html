Package 03

Intranet, Knowledge & Marketing

The company intranet, rebuilt and modernized inside Flight Deck. Not just the SOP library, the whole thing: links and tools, the knowledge base, people, onboarding, the marketing and brand library, the vendor and license portal, and the company calendar. One place, so the team stops toggling between SharePoint, Microsoft 365, Monday, Canva, and a WhatsApp channel.

01 / Overview

The brief

Today the company's internal knowledge and operations are spread across a SharePoint intranet, Microsoft 365, Monday, Canva, and even a WhatsApp channel for scheduling. The current SharePoint intranet was built by someone outside the skill set: content is duplicated, dated, and hard to navigate. This package rebuilds it modern inside Flight Deck so the team never has to leave the platform to find what it needs.

The SOP library is one part of a bigger whole. The intranet also carries the links and tools the team uses every day (JetHQ links, industry tools, and listing platforms), the crew with an org chart, roles, and tenure, the onboarding and general biz-ops flows currently stuck in Monday, the marketing and brand asset library unified from Microsoft 365 and Canva, a vendor and license portal for the solutions engineers and internal IT, and the primary company calendar. Sensitive HR and compensation data stays on OneDrive with leadership. A summer intern is lined up to help with the migration.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • The SharePoint intranet rebuilt and modernized inside Flight Deck, one place the team never leaves.
  • Links and tools in one navigable place: JetHQ links, industry tools, and listing platforms.
  • A searchable knowledge and SOP library migrated from Microsoft 365.
  • The crew: an org chart, roles, and tenure.
  • Onboarding and general biz-ops flows rebuilt off Monday.
  • The marketing and brand asset library unified from Microsoft 365 and Canva, brand book front and center.
  • A vendor and license portal with renewal dates, contract links, and platform links, for the solutions engineers and IT.
  • The primary company calendar, replacing the Microsoft calendar and WhatsApp scheduling.
