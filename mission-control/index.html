Workspace · 01

Mission Control

Mission Control is the page everyone at JetHQ opens first. A role-based heads-up display where each person lands on the KPIs, shortcuts, and widgets that match how they work. It is web and mobile responsive from day one, and installs to the phone home screen like an app.

01 / Overview

The brief

Today the team bounces between their computer, the Hub, the SharePoint intranet, Salesforce, and Monday to get through a day. Every jump is a login and a context switch. Mission Control replaces that with one landing page, custom per person by role. Leadership sees company health, a broker sees active listings and deals against goal, a sales engineer sees requests.

Home is the front door to everything else in Flight Deck, and it reaches the Hub in one click without leaving the surface. Design quality matters here. The surface should feel modern and considered, complementary to the black-and-white Hub.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • Each user lands on a home built for their role, with the KPIs and widgets that match the work they do.
  • Pin shortcuts are customizable per person: the Hub, active listings, delivery tracking, and more, arranged the way each user wants them.
  • An active-listings widget sits front and center because it is the most-used view.
  • A deals-against-goal widget keeps the company target in view and shows progress toward it.
  • A Hub quick-access button reaches the Hub in one click without leaving the surface.
  • A calendar widget shows who is in and out this week, and a live-activity feed shows what is happening across the company.
  • Web and mobile responsive from day one, installable to the phone home screen, so the team works from anywhere.

03 / Scope

Scope of work

Stage 1 · Q3 2026

Shell and role-based home

Stand up the Flight Deck surface and get the core home live for every role.

  • Stand up the Flight Deck shell and the role-based home.

Personalization and role-based KPIs

Make the home each person's own and tune what each role sees.

Stage 3 · Q3 2026

Widget library and saved layouts

Broaden what Home can show and let each person keep their own arrangement.
