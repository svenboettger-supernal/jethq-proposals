Proposal for JetHQ

Our partnership

JetHQ Flight Deck

One company operating system that mirrors the prototype. The Hub stays the single source for listings; Flight Deck links to it and brings everything else, from the home screen and the intranet to sales and finance, into one place. Web and mobile responsive, built across Q3 and Q4 2026, priced as a menu you choose from.

The menu

Six build packages, priced monthly on the point-based plan. Q3 2026 stands up the core the whole team lives in every day, priced now. The three Q4 workstreams, Sales Ops, Admin and Finance, and Ask JetHQ, are scoped and priced separately, to be confirmed. Pricing below is indicative.
