The menu

Six build packages on the JetHQ point-based plan. Together they take JetHQ from tools scattered across the Hub, Salesforce, SharePoint, and Monday to one operating system the whole company runs on. Core & Home comes first as the foundation, and everything after it can be switched on in the order that suits JetHQ. Each package shows its build time and monthly investment. Pricing is indicative, and Admin & Finance is sized together with Johnny.
