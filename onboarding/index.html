Workspace · 05

Onboarding

The onboarding and general biz-ops flows that live in Monday today, rebuilt inside Flight Deck where the team will actually use them. Nobody likes Monday, so this is a low-effort, high-value early win.

01 / Overview

The brief

Onboarding and a set of general biz-ops flows run in Monday today, and the team does not like or use Monday. The goal is to bring those flows into Flight Deck: a clean onboarding checklist and the recurring operational flows the team runs, in the platform they already open.

It is kept light on purpose. This is the everyday flows the team needs, not a heavy project-management system.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • A clear onboarding flow for new hires, so every start follows the same path.
  • The general biz-ops flows migrated out of Monday and into Flight Deck.
  • Tasks carry an owner and a due date, so nothing sits unassigned.
  • A checklist that runs itself, with steps that trigger in order.
  • Pairs with the People Hub, so onboarding and the team directory stay in step.
  • A named operator owns the flows and keeps them current.
