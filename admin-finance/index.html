Workspace · 10

Admin & Finance

Accounts payable and receivable and the finance operations that today run on some of the most expensive roles in the company. The largest workstream in the plan, scoped closely with Supernal.

01 / Overview

The brief

Finance is the biggest-ticket module. Accounts payable and receivable are among the most expensive functions the company staffs. Done well, this module carries real weight, so the approach is scoped closely with Supernal before build.

The detail is defined together, since finance is specific to how JetHQ runs. This page frames the shape, and the exact scope is set in the working sessions.

02 / Objectives

What success looks like

  • Accounts payable and receivable live in one place inside Flight Deck.
  • The finance flows that run the business sit on a single surface, not spread across tools.
  • A clean view of what is owed and owing, always current.
  • The manual finance effort is reduced, so the expensive roles spend less time on repetitive work.
  • The approach is defined together with Supernal before anything is built.
  • A named finance owner carries the module and signs off the scope.

03 / Scope

Scope of work

Stage 1 · Phase 4

Define the approach and map the flows

Define the finance approach with Supernal and map the accounts payable and receivable flows.

  • Work through the finance approach with Supernal in the scoping sessions.
  • Map the accounts payable and receivable flows as they run today.
  • Confirm the accounting system details and the connectors that matter.
  • Agree the shape of the build before any of it is committed.

Stage 2 · Phase 4

Core payable and receivable flows live

The core payable and receivable flows live in Flight Deck, with a clean view of what is owed and owing.

  • Core accounts payable and receivable flows live inside Flight Deck.
  • A clean view of what is owed and owing, current across the module.
  • The finance flows that run the business sit on one surface.
  • A named finance owner working in the module day to day.

Stage 3 · Phase 4

Automation and accounting connectors

Automate the repetitive finance work, with connectors to the accounting system as agreed.

  • Automate the repetitive finance work that runs on manual effort today.
  • Connectors to the accounting system, such as QuickBooks, as agreed.
  • Reduce the manual load on the most expensive roles.
  • Tune the flows against how the finance owner actually works.
